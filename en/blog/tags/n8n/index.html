Vibe Sparking

For deep reasoning, multimodal processing, and complex coding, Gemini 2.5 Pro Deep Think is the strongest contender; For comprehensive research and automated information synthesis into reports, choose ChatGPT Deep Research; For terminal-based AI assistance with writing and debugging large project codebases, Claude Code is your most reliable choice; For IDE-based collaborative bug fixing and feature development like working with a teammate, Cursor better fits development workflows; For image creation, use Midjourney; for short video production, rely on Veo 3; For workflow automation and script process interaction, let n8n run autonomously.

Section titled “I. ChatGPT o3 (Top Choice for AI Q&A)”

Section titled “II. ChatGPT GPT‑4.5 (Best for AI Writing)”

For creating articles, writing content, and crafting complex text summaries, GPT‑4.5 possesses superior comprehension and expression capabilities. Rich in emotion, clear in structure, diverse in language—ideal for copywriting, reports, or long-form content.

Section titled “III. Gemini 2.5 Pro (Long Text / Advanced Reasoning)”

Gemini 2.5 Pro is Google’s flagship model, supporting multimodal input including text, images, audio, video, and PDFs. It excels in complex reasoning, code generation, advanced mathematics, and scientific problems, achieving high scores in mathematical or scientific tests. Anthropic’s Claude Code is a command-line terminal AI programming assistant. It can automatically map your project structure, understand codebases, analyze dependencies, and within seconds generate documentation, add new features, or refactor code. Section titled “VII. Cursor (AI Programming IDE)”

Section titled “VIII. Veo 3 (AI Video Generation)”

Google’s latest Veo 3 model excels at generating high-quality short videos, supporting text or image input to generate content with original soundtracks, voiceovers, background music, or dialogue, with precise lip-sync, outputting up to 1080p resolution. Section titled “IX. n8n (AI Agent / Automation Workflows)”

n8n provides a workflow automation platform that, combined with AI Agents, can build complex workflows: automatically scraping web pages, connecting to databases, calling APIs, letting AI make decisions and execute tasks while continuously operating within systems. Suitable for enterprise scenarios like customer service bots, data processing workflows, and scheduled reminders.

Competing with n8n, Dify, Coze? Google Opal Generates AI Workflows with One Sentence

Aug 1, 2025
Vibe Sparking

Google strikes again! Opal brings the magical “write one sentence → drag blocks” operation to AI workflows, creating functional mini-apps without spending a dime. Less than a week after launch, it’s already sparking heated discussions that n8n/Zapier might lose sleep over: describe your needs → AI automatically chains together Gemini models, search, and external tools, then gives you a visual flowchart that you can drag and adjust anytime, plus one-click sharing with colleagues.

What Exactly is Opal?

Section titled “What Exactly is Opal?”
  • Google Labs’ brand-new experimental product, featuring a dual mode of “natural language + visualization,” currently 100% free and limited to US account beta testing.
  • Positioned as an AI mini-app builder: automatically arranges input boxes, prompts, Gemini model calls, tool APIs, and other steps into workflow nodes.

You describe: “Turn meeting notes into action items.” Opal instantly generates four nodes: “Upload notes → Gemini summary → Generate todos → Output email,” making the workflow crystal clear.

Five Major Highlights

Section titled “Five Major Highlights”

1. Pure Conversational Workflow Generation

Section titled “1. Pure Conversational Workflow Generation”

Natural language alone can connect multiple models and tools, eliminating the need for code and YAML.

2. Drag-and-Drop Visual Editing

Section titled “2. Drag-and-Drop Visual Editing”

Want to insert new steps or modify prompts? Simply drag blocks or type another instruction.

3. Templates & Remix

Section titled “3. Templates & Remix”

Official Starter Gallery comes built-in, with templates for blog writers, thumbnail generators, data scrapers, and more—ready to grab and modify.

4. One-Click Publish & Share

Section titled “4. One-Click Publish & Share”

After generating a link, colleagues can experience it using their own Google accounts, facilitating collaborative testing.

5. Completely Free + No Deployment Required

Section titled “5. Completely Free + No Deployment Required”

Currently runs on Google’s backend, no need to maintain your own servers, perfect for idea validation and internal team demos.
