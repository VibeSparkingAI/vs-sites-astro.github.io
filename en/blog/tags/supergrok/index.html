xAI Grok gets Companions feature, adding animated characters to voice conversations

Section titled “xAI Grok gets Companions feature, adding animated characters to voice conversations”

According to the information, the Grok companions feature is available only for Premium+ or SuperGrok subscribers. Meanwhile, the developers haven’t announced this feature rollout for Android.

xAI is rolling out Companions, a new feature for the Grok app, which brings animated characters for users who want to make their conversations more interactive. The feature is part of the new Grok iOS app update released today on the App Store.

A companion could best fall in the category of AI personality, and these are fused within the Grok app. These personalities are an addition to the voice mode but with a dedicated space, where the visualized character can move and react according to the conversation.
